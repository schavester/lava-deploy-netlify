ARTBOTS
Sculptures by Liz Mamorsky

Opening Second Saturday Gallery Night
September 11, from 7 to 10 pm
Open by appointment until Sept. 28

Future Studio Gallery welcomes Liz Mamorsky back for her third solo show at the Highland Park gallery. Liz’s ARTBOTS are culled from the detritus of technology, a never-ending stream of electronic waste. The speed of technological redundancy is astounding. No sooner is a new product created than it is rendered obsolete, and Liz is the recipient of a small portion of that waste, using it to create her sculptural wall pieces. As always, a small quantity of free artist trading cards will be available at the opening.

â€œI cannot make sense of waste, but I can make art of waste, and am driven to do so. I like to think that my anthropomorphic ARTBOTS and other creatures are helping to make the world just a little bit cleaner . . . and certainly a lot more fun!â€
