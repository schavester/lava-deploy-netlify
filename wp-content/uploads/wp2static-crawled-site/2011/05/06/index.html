LAVA event review

LAVA’s April 2011 Sunday Salon video

Richard Schave
0 1.1k
Share

If you missed the Salon festivities at Clifton’s on April 24, you’re in luck, because the cameras were rolling, or whatever it is cameras do these days. Tune in to the long video to enjoy Ian Whitcomb’s tales of how his improbable British Invasion pop star youth translated into a life spent in Lotusland, followed by the musical stylings of our beloved human muppet The Ukulady (link to the start of her set), accompanied by her Evil Sandwiches for a special Easter Sunday set of tunes and patter. And if you’ve only got a few minutes, we recommend Ian Whitcomb’s Love Letter to Los Angeles, definitely a Sunday Salon highlight. Join us again on May 29 for yet another extraordinary Sunday Salon, upstairs at Clifton’s (where all the smart kids go).
