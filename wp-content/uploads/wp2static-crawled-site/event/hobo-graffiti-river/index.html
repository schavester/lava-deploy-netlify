Hobo Graffiti

Kim Cooper
0 1.7k
Share

We pull your sleeve to an interesting non-LAVA excursion as anthropologist Susan Phillips partners with the Los Angeles Department of Cultural Affairs to present a Hobo Graffiti River Walk during CURRENT:LA Public Art Biennial.

The Hobo Graffiti River Walk goes to the confluence of the Los Angeles River and the Arroyo Seco, and a site where Susan has located a remarkable collection of century-old hobo graffiti.

There is limited capacity and RSVPs are required to attend. Click the “buy tickets” link above to be taken to the CURRENT:LA reservation page. Map link does not show precise event location, which will be provided to registered attendees.
