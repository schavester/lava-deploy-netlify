McSweeney’s Issue #34 Launch Party at 826LA and Stories<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

Pick up your reserved copy of McSweeney’s Quarterly #34 at the Echo Park Time Travel Mart<\/p>\n

enjoy food and drink at Stories<\/p>\n

stay for a discussion & signing with McSweeney’s<\/a> contributors Nick McDonell<\/a> and Teddy Wayne<\/a> in the 826LA writing lab<\/p>\n

with musical guest, The Blasting Company<\/a><\/p>\n

Issue 34 features new stories of shipwrecks, kidnappings and bad vacations, by (among others) Anthony Doerr, Daniel Handler, and T. C. Boyle. Also included are new letters about wine and Hawaii from John Hodgman and Sarah Vowell, twenty-one dead-on self-portraits drawn by the likes of Michael Martone, Michel Gondry, and Sarah Silverman, and, beyond all this, in a standalone volume, Nick McDonell’s stunning exploration of the latest iteration of the war in Iraq–a ground-level account from within the 1st Cavalry Division. The whole thing weighs in at just under 400 pages, and comes in its own custom-made double-sleeve.<\/p>\n

RSVP & reserve your copy here<\/a>.<\/p>\n

826LA East \/ The Echo Park Time Travel Mart & Stories
1714 & 1716 W Sunset Blvd.
Los Angeles, California 90026
Monday, April 26th, 2010
7:30 p.m.<\/p>\n

FREE to attend<\/p>\n
