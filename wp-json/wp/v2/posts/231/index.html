GHOULA<\/strong> meets for cocktails in haunted places on the 13th of each month. \u00e2\u20ac\u0153SPIRITS with SPIRITS<\/strong>\u00e2\u20ac\u009d is a casual gathering of regional ghost hunters and those that just like ghost stories. Open to all, from the curious skeptic to the passionate phantom pursuer. Make friends, and toast a ghost! Let’s put the \u00e2\u20ac\u0153Boo!\u00e2\u20ac\u009d back into \u00e2\u20ac\u0153booze.\u00e2\u20ac\u009d All those who attend will receive a free G.H.O.U.L.A. button. If you already have one, please wear it so others can find you.<\/p>\n

This month’s meeting will be held at the historic (and haunted) Aztec Hotel<\/strong>, one of the iconic roadside attractions of old Route 66. In addition to its place in Southern California’s car culture, it is also said to have been (over the decades) a brothel, a speak-easy, a gambling den, a mobster hangout, and a half-way house. The management has agreed to give attendees tours of haunted room 120, where an actress (or possibly a prostitute) died when her head cracked open on the radiator during the throws of passion.  In addition, there will be themed live entertainment in the bar, and some other spooky surprises.<\/p>\n
