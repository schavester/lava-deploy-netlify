LAVA Visionary and president of the LA chapter of the Dorothy Parker Society, Adrienne Crew will host a short walking tour of F. Scott Fitzgerald’s (West) Hollywood and the places that were significant to him at the end of the writer’s life. The tour will begin at the corner of Sunset Blvd & Crescent Heights (exact details furnished upon registration) and conclude at Greenblatt’s Deli, where Sheilah Graham purchased the Hershey bar which was the last thing Fitzgerald ate.<\/p>\n

A partial list of both extant and demolished locations along the route are: The Garden of Allah apartments, <\/span>Schwab’s Drug store and t<\/span>he apartment of Sheilah Graham.<\/span><\/p>\n

Encore walking tour to augment Dorothy Parker Society activities<\/a> in December, starting December 12, 2013 with a cocktail party celebrating the publication of Under the Table: A Dorothy Parker Cocktail Guide<\/a><\/em> at the Los Angeles Athletic Club. Reserve<\/a> your spot now. <\/p>\n

$15 per person. Pre-payment and reservation required. Tour location shall be sent after confirmed reservation. Contact Adrienne Crew atat info@adriennecrew.com for information about reservations and payments. Paypal and credit cards accepted. <\/span>Comfortable walking shoes and sunscreen are advised. <\/span>Plan on arriving 15 minutes prior to tour start time for check-in. <\/span><\/p>\n
