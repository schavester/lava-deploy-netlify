On April 22, 2012, LAVA – The Los Angeles Visionaries Association invited the public  to an Open House at the historic home\/gallery of LAVA Visionary April Dammann and her husband Ron Dammann, president of Stendahl Galleries, a participating gallery in Pacific Standard Time.<\/p>

April gave informal gallery tours and spoke about her new book, Exhibitionist: Earl Stendahl, Art Dealer as Impresario<\/a>,<\/em> which chronicles the life and times of her husband\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s grandfather, a pioneering art dealer in Los Angeles. Exhibitionist made book critic David Kipen\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s top ten list of \u00e2\u20ac\u0153Best Books\u00e2\u20ac\u009d published in California in 2011. See scenes from the day here<\/a>.<\/p>
