    \n
  1.  View historic artifacts on display mentioned in “The Help,” “The Butler,” “Warmth of Other Suns,” “12 Years A Slave,” and other films by and about the African and African American experience.<\/span><\/li>\n
  2. Enjoy a screening of private home movies taken by African Americans, including black cowboys, parades and footage of home movies from the vault of 20s aviatrix and entertainer Marie Coker Dickinson.<\/span><\/li>\n
