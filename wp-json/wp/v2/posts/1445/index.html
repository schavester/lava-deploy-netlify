LAVA Visionary A. Laura Brody and Opulent Mobility want your art and inventions!<\/p>\n

Wheelchairs, walkers, prosthetic limbs, crutches and other assistive technology devices are part of our lives. But in the grand scheme of design innovations they’ve been left in the dust. There are hundreds of thousands of designs for chairs, eyeglasses and technology of so many other kinds. Now is your chance to help change the conversation about the assistive technology of the future.<\/p>\n

Please submit your art, designs and inventions dealing with mobility, disability and accessibility.<\/p>\n

Deadline for submission is June 30th, 2015 at 5 PM PST. The exhibit runs from September 10-19th, 2015 at California State University, Northridge’s West Gallery. Opulent Mobility’s opening reception will be September 12, 2015 from 4-8 PM.<\/p>\n

Submit online at http:\/\/www.dreamsbymachine.com\/opulent-mobility-a-call-for-art-and-ideas\/<\/a>.<\/p>\n

We’re excited to see your ideas!<\/p>\n
