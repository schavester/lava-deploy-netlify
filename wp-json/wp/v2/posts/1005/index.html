Adrienne Crew will host a short walking tour of F. Scott Fitzgerald’s (West) Hollywood and the places that were significant to him at the end of the writer’s life. The tour will begin at the corner of Sunset Blvd & Crescent Heights (exact details furnished upon registration) and conclude at Greenblatt’s Deli, where Sheilah Graham purchased the Hershey bar which was the last thing Fitzgerald ate.<\/p>\n

A partial list of both extant and demolished locations along the route are:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • The Garden of Allah apartments <\/li>\n
  • Schwab’s Drug store <\/li>\n
  • The apartment of Sheilah Graham<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Special guest speakers Martin Turnbull<\/a> & Marc Chevalier, presenters the next day at the June LAVA<\/span> Sunday Salon<\/a>, will be on hand during the first 30 minutes(s) of each tour to enhance our understanding of several of the locations covered on the tour.<\/p>\n

    Comfortable walking shoes and sunscreen are advised.<\/p>\n

    $15 per person. Pre-payment and reservation required. Tour location shall be sent after confirmed reservation. Contact Adrienne Crew atat info@adriennecrew.com for information about reservations and payments. Paypal and checks accepted.<\/p>\n

    Plan on arriving 15 minutes prior to tour start time for check-in.<\/p>\n
