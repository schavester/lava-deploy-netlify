This tour focuses on Bukowski\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s great passions: writing, screwing and Los Angeles. We\u00e2\u20ac\u2122ll take in the canonical locations of his life and myth: the Postal Annex Terminal where he gathered the material for \u00e2\u20ac\u0153Post Office,\u00e2\u20ac\u009d the De Longpre apartment where he briefly experimented with marriage and fatherhood, one of his favorite bars and liquor stores, and many other spots. Along the way, we\u00e2\u20ac\u2122ll explore the people and ideas that made up the warp and weft of Buk\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s rich inner life. This Esotouric bus adventure is hosted by Richard Schave, one of the organizers behind the successful campaign to name Charles Bukowski’s East Hollywood bungalow a Cultural-Historic Landmark.<\/p>\n

“Haunts of a Dirty Old Man: Charles Bukowski’s LA” spans Bukowski’s personal city, from Skid Row to once-genteel Crown Hill, to Bukowski’s favorite East Hollywood liquor store, the Pink Elephant.<\/p>\n
