The Whisky & Poetry Salon is an intimate evening of Scotland’s finest single malts and the communal love of all things poetic….<\/p>\n

Bring a poem, your own or someone else’s, and read in our candlelit 1920’s loft with a dramatic city skyscape in downtown Los Angeles.<\/p>\n

Our cheeky and erudite whisky ambassador Johnnie “the Scot” Mundell dispenses top-drawer whisky & education. Light bites will be served.<\/p>\n

Performance poet Traci Akemi Kato-kiriyama will be our dynamic finale.<\/p>\n

We can’t wait to trade you whisky for your words….<\/p>\n
