No’a Winter Lazerus utilizes a wide range of skills in pursuit of his artistic vision and expression.<\/span><\/span><\/span> <\/span><\/span><\/p>\n

No’a creates for film, television, visual art, installation art, music recording projects and live performances. His work has been tied to the films 300 and Watchmen, to films for PBS and he is Grammy nominated for his work in music recording and production.<\/span><\/span><\/span><\/p>\n

No’a is known by his peers as a musical artist who can go any direction creatively with originality, innovation and a unique sonic approach. From Indie and Alt to Neo-Classical to Ambient and Spectral Sound Sculpting, he is recognized for dancing with many genres, instruments and styles.<\/span><\/span><\/span><\/p>\n

No’a is currently working on projects with several known artists from Ladytron and Sonja Drakulich to Sonos and Bickram Ghosh. He is also completing a new radical musical called \u00e2\u20ac\u0153Ian Forest\u00e2\u20ac\u009d as part of his Masters Thesis in Consciousness Studies and continues working on his own recording projects and new scoring projects all done in his home studio, \u00e2\u20ac\u0153Voice of the Silence Sound\u00e2\u20ac\u009d.<\/span><\/span><\/span><\/p>\n

No’a is co-creator of the Art of Sound label marrying artists and artwork with composers and musicians, he has completed a new project called \u00e2\u20ac\u0153Rad Files\u00e2\u20ac\u009d for the Art of Sound label and explains it as –<\/span><\/span><\/span> <\/span><\/p>\n

\u00e2\u20ac\u0153<\/span><\/span>An exploration into the distortions of beauty, the rhythms of chance and the answers just our of reach\u00e2\u20ac\u009d.<\/span><\/span><\/span><\/p>\n

Live No’a can be seen at unique events as one half of \u00e2\u20ac\u0153The Housepainters\u00e2\u20ac\u009d along with artist Louis Cannizzaro. Louis paints found objects while No’a creates with found sounds.<\/span><\/span><\/span> <\/span><\/p>\n
