The Stendahl Galleries of Hollywood presents the opening reception for \u00e2\u20ac\u0153LOOKING BOXES\u00e2\u20ac\u009d Playful Ways of Seeing the World by artist and industrial designer Maynard Hale Lyndon.<\/p>\n

Boxes and accompanying books for sale.<\/p>\n

Maynard Hale Lyndon\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s design legacy took root in Boston with Design Research, the store that brought modern living to American homes. On the West Coast, he founded an award-winning toy store (Santa Monica, 1967) and directed product development at Forms&Surfaces (Santa Barbara).<\/p>\n

On view: a choreography of memory and impression: \u00e2\u20ac\u0153Looking Boxes are my layered constructions, wherein I juxtapose images from the thousands of photographs I have taken, (many in Los Angeles) stretching back to 1962. The boxes combine details of places in surprising and serendipitous scenes. Each is one-of-a-kind. There are no duplicates.\u00e2\u20ac\u009d Custom boxes on special order, with the collector\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s own photographs.<\/p>\n

The Stendahl Galleries celebrates one hundred years of continuous operation and is a proud participant in Pacific Standard Time.<\/p>\n
