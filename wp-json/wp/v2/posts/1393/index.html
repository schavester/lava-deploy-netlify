Join LAVA Visionary and art historian Dr. Paul Koudounaris<\/a>, author of “Heavenly Bodies: Cult Treasures and Spectacular Saints from the Catacombs<\/a>” and “The Empire of Death: A Cultural History of Ossuaries and Charnel Houses<\/a>” for a very special presentation inaugurating the relaunch of the monthly LAVA<\/span> Sunday Salon at Library Bar<\/a>.<\/p>\n

Demonically-Possessed Cats with Dr. Paul Koudounaris<\/strong><\/p>\n

Does your cat do things like:<\/p>\n

spit fire?<\/p>\n

speak in tongues?<\/p>\n

levitate?<\/p>\n

shape shift?<\/p>\n

wantonly kill people for no apparent reason?<\/p>\n

This behavior may be more than simple bad kitty syndrome\u00e2\u20ac\u201dyour cat may be possessed by a demon. But don\u00e2\u20ac\u2122t worry, you\u00e2\u20ac\u2122re not alone. In fact, there is an entire history of demonically-possessed cats. Felines were once considered by theologians to be easy prey for demons, who could enter their bodies and wreck incredible havoc on mankind. And did you know\u00e2\u20ac\u00a6 demonically-possessed cats are still believed to be with us today?! Dr. Paul Koudounaris has immersed himself in the study of demonic cats, in order to to give a talk on this sadly neglected topic. Cat lovers will like this lecture, and cat haters will like it even more. Neither side will ever look at a domestic cat the same way again.<\/p>\n

Please note this lecture has been given by Dr. Koudounaris in abbreviated forms about town, but this only<\/em> time he will present the full lecture in Los Angeles.<\/p>\n
