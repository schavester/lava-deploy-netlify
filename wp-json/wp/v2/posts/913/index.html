Roar Shack is a collective of writers and artists who come together each month. Some of us come from fiction, some from memoir, some from poetry, and music and performance and just about anything that leaves its own blood on the page. Our evenings are festive and dare we say, indelible. Authors read, musicians play, and we host a writing improv contest that’s more fun than ought to be legal.<\/p>\n

Our January 13 lineup continues to draw from the deepest pools of creative talent:  NYT Bestselling author Jami Attenberg, Natashia Deon, Belo Miguel Cipriani, Zoe Ruiz interviewing Literary Death Match founder Adrian Todd Zuniga, December Live Write winner Daniel Safarik, the Live Write contest featuring Antonia Crane and Sofia Gil, and musical guests The Phonetic Disasters.<\/p>\n
