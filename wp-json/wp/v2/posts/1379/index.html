Fine single malt whisky and dynamic, star-power poets at a classic Hollywood lounge?<\/p>\n

Yes, please.<\/p>\n

Celebrate the power of whisky and words at the Whisky & Poetry Salon’s Two Year Anniversary<\/a><\/strong> on WEDNESDAY, Nov 19 from 7:00 to 9:30 p.m. at the Rat Pack-retro-fabulous 3 Clubs Lounge<\/a> in Hollywood!<\/p>\n

The celebration honors WritLargePress’<\/a>s recent, epic DTLAB #90for90<\/a> literary pop-up at Union Station with a trio of amazing Writ Large Press poets: Melora Walters<\/a>,  Mike “thePoet” Sonksen<\/a>, and a rare appearance by Chiwan Choi<\/a>.<\/p>\n

Riding the wave of pre-<\/span>Whisky Fest<\/strong> <\/span>excitement, the cheeky and erudite single malt specialist<\/span> <\/span>Johnnie “the Scot” Mundell <\/a>will be in top form dispensing fine single malt Scotch and expert whisky education.<\/span><\/p>\n

Finally, you will DEFINITELY want to come early\/stay late to investigate the Three Clubs cocktail menu revamped by<\/span> <\/span>Michael J. Neff<\/a>, one of the most innovative pioneers of the craft cocktail movement.<\/span><\/p>\n

We drink poetry…come celebrate with us.<\/p>\n
