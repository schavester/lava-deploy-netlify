ARTBOTS
Sculptures by Liz Mamorsky<\/p>\n

Opening Second Saturday Gallery Night
September 11, from 7 to 10 pm
Open by appointment until Sept. 28<\/p>\n

Future Studio Gallery welcomes Liz Mamorsky back for her third solo show at the Highland Park gallery. Liz’s ARTBOTS are culled from the detritus of technology, a never-ending stream of electronic waste. The speed of technological redundancy is astounding. No sooner is a new product created than it is rendered obsolete, and Liz is the recipient of a small portion of that waste, using it to create her sculptural wall pieces. As always, a small quantity of free artist trading cards will be available at the opening.<\/p>\n

\u00e2\u20ac\u0153I cannot make sense of waste, but I can make art of waste, and am driven to do so. I like to think that my anthropomorphic ARTBOTS and other creatures are helping to make the world just a little bit cleaner . . . and certainly a lot more fun!\u00e2\u20ac\u009d<\/p>\n

Future Studio Gallery is home to Chicken Boy, the Statue of Liberty of Los Angeles, a permanent art installation atop the roof of the gallery. Each month, a free Chicken Boy trading card ia also available at the art opening. NELAart.com Second Saturday Gallery Night features some 20+ art spaces in Highland Park, Eagle Rock, Cypress Park, and Mt Washington.<\/p>\n
