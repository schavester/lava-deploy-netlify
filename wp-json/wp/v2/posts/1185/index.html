Our second <\/span>All About<\/em> in 2014 will be the long-awaited FIRST public tour of the Theatre at Ace Hotel, formally known as the Uniter Artists Theatre!<\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/div>\n
The historic United Artists Theatre was designed as a “cathedral to the movies” and we are thrilled to see it once again in the hands of people who plan to operate it as a theatre for live and cinematic arts. Join us on February 1st to see the original theater as well as some of the spaces restored by Ace Hotel.  <\/span> <\/span><\/div>\n

<\/span><\/div>\n
Warnings:<\/em><\/strong> While the main auditorium level is easily accessible, most of the walking tours around the theatre involve a lot of stairs and tight spaces. Some areas are elevator-accessible, and we will accommodate patrons needing assistance as much as we can.<\/em><\/span><\/div>\n
