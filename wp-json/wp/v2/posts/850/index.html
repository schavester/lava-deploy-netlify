Join us for a transformative weekend of Mask Making on the Ocean in Malibu.<\/p>\n

Facilitated by powerful instructors, Ceylan Hulya MA in Transformative Arts and Certified Teacher with Kaleo Ching Methodology<\/a> and Award-Winning Artist and Performer Eva Montealegre<\/p>\n

Through guided meditation, lucid dream work, movement, and mask making you will discover your own personal mythology and totem. This is a perfect workshop to bring a friend to as you and your partner will be making each others mask. In the process you will find that Mask making is a fun, kinesthetic, and creative approach for healing and personal transformation. Also, mask making is a powerful way of giving expression to parts of yourself that don\u00e2\u20ac\u2122t normally get expressed.<\/p>\n

Dates: October 27th and 28th <\/p>\n

Where: Art 4 All People in Malibu (Oceanside)<\/p>\n

Time:  October 27th 11:00am- 5:00pm, October 28th. 12:00 pm-5:00pm<\/p>\n

What: Guided Visualization, Movement, Masking Making, Transformation as your Mask becomes your Totem with variety mixed media Art Supplies.<\/p>\n

Participants: Maximum 14<\/p>\n

All Art Supplies will be provided. Please bring a bag lunch to enjoy the amazing vista of Mother Ocean and wear comfortable clothes.<\/p>\n
