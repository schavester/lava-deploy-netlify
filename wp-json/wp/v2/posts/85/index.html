Gabriel Aldaz is an engineer by day, and muffler man tracker by, well, when he has the time. Over the course of several years, Gabriel tracked down various Paul Bunyan\/muffler men\/tall guy statues, discovering their histories. He compiled the stories of over twenty big guys that he visited. We met him when he tracked down Chicken Boy.<\/p>\n

This exhibit will feature photos from his journeys, and also a book launch for the book of the same name and subject. And speaking of journeys, he’s since moved to Denmark, so he will be our \u00e2\u20ac\u0153artist who traveled the furthest to show at Future Studio Gallery.\u00e2\u20ac\u009d A title that we imagine he will hold forever.<\/p>\n
