Christina Galante has been the retail and events manager at Los Angeles non-profit 826LA<\/a> for the past 3 years. Founded by Dave Eggers, 826LA offers free after-school tutoring and creative writing programs to students 6-18 and is one of eight locations across the US.<\/p>\n

Originally from New York, Christina lived in Manhattan and Brooklyn for 10+ years and made her way through the garment, publishing, advertising\/communications\/PR and retail industries before moving California in 2005. After totally missing the mark and spending three lost years in San Diego, she made her way to Los Angeles in 2008.<\/p>\n

Most days you can either find her in the Echo Park Time Travel Mart slinging Mammoth Chunks<\/a> and time machine fuel or planning fundraisers like the Echo Park Lake Paddle Boat Regatta and Chickens in Love<\/a> (a full length record with songs written by kids and performed by LA bands).<\/p>\n
