The Los Angeles Historic Theatre Foundation will be conducting its March “All About” docu-tours at the newly renovated Saban Theatre in Beverly Hills, which was formerly known as The Wilshire or the Fox Wilshire.  Doors will open at 10am and the event will include a presentation on this history of the theatre, which opened its doors on September 19th, 1930 as the Fox Wilshire and 1981 was converted into a live performance venue and renamed The Wilshire. <\/p>\n

Following the presentation will be a inclusive basement to projection booth tour that will include numerous areas that are usually not accessible to the general public!  FREE!<\/p>\n
