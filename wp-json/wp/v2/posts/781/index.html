Divide and collide\u00e2\u20ac\u00a6Heidi Duckler Dance Theatre (HDDT) will capture the attention and ignite the imaginations of travelers with the company\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s new work Kiss n\u00e2\u20ac\u2122Ride. Following star-crossed lovers to their unanticipated departure, the performance will draw upon the futuristic architecture of the location and bring a humanistic touch to the modern structure.<\/p>\n

By exploring the notion of leaving as a beginning and end, Kiss n\u00e2\u20ac\u2122Ride will invigorate the otherwise functional site with a temporary public art project that is commissioned by Los Angeles\u00e2\u20ac\u2122 Department of Cultural Affairs and Los Angeles World Airports. Folding chairs in tow, the audience will experience the Van Nuys FlyAway and witness an intricate story of travelers against the ongoing backdrop of the random entrances and exits of commuters.<\/p>\n

Through electric choreography directed by Heidi Duckler, LED-infused costumes, new video design by artist Ray Sun, and a cutting-edge live-DJ, HDDT will transform the FlyAway into a kiss and ride away from the daily life…<\/p>\n

This event is FREE to the public.<\/p>\n
