LAVA Visionary David Caldwell presents his War Child Show on the evening of LAVA’s monthly Sunday Salon. Enjoy yourself downtown until nightfall, then head over to Caldwell’s Arts District loft, where there will be wine and snacks and a most unusual multi-media art presentation. Doors open 7:00pm for this free event, and the show starts around 8pm. No one will be admitted after the show begins.<\/p>\n

War Child is a Monumental Sculpture.
\nAn Installation in the form of,
\na Magically Elaborate Altar.
\n14 feet high, 40 feet wide, and 26 feet deep,
\nIt gives Birth to a 40 minute Experience.
\nA Video, Sound, and Light Show Performance.
\nThe Video Juxtaposes Manipulated Images,
\nOf Nature, Civilization, Creation, and Destruction.
\nWar Childs 3 Dimensional and Technical Wizardry,
\nEntice the Audience on a Journey.
\nThe Paradigm of Duality,
\nThat Shapes Human Existence.
\nA Synthesis of Multiple Systems,
\nWar Child becomes a Living,
\nBreathing, Speaking, Organic Entity.
\nAll of the Technology is Embedded,
\nand Fully Integrated into the Performance.
\nThe Living Installation Impacts,
\nSpace, Sight, Sound, and Memory.
\nA Shifting, Constantly Changing Environment,
\nEnvelops the Audience,
\nand Transports Them,
\nto Another Dimension.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n
