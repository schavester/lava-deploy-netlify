On Saturday, July 24, the second World Peace Interfaith Pilgrimage<\/span><\/strong> to Mount Baldy will take place in the gorgeous San Gabriel Mountains, just 50 miles east of Los Angeles. <\/span><\/span><\/p>\n

Mount<\/span><\/span> Baldy is a Holy Mountain and has been recognized as such by the local Gabrielino\/Tungva Native Americans for generations. Since 1959, this mountain has also been considered holy by Members of the non-profit spiritual organization, The Aetherius Society, who recognize this sacred mountain as having been charged with Cosmic energies. Each summer since 1959, we have been visiting the mountain to send out powerful waves of spiritual energy to the world for World Peace and Global Healing through the action of selfless prayer. The first World Peace Pilgrimage was held in August 2009 to mark the 50th anniversary of the charging of this Holy Mountain. The event was one of just 60 “Pre-Parliament Events” around the world endorsed by the Parliament of the World\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s Religions<\/span><\/span><\/span><\/span><\/a>, the largest interfaith movement in the world, prior to their gathering in Melbourne, Australia in December, 2009. Over 200 people from various religious traditions, and no religious traditions, gathered on the mountain sending a wave of prayer energy to the world. We hope you will join us to make this second World Peace Pilgrimage an even greater success. <\/span><\/span><\/p>\n

Full details of the arrangements and logistics are contained on the website: www.mysticbaldy.org<\/span><\/span><\/span><\/span><\/a> or else you may call (323) 465-9652. <\/span><\/span><\/p>\n
