Today the Cameo theater at 528 South Broadway celebrates its 100 birthday.<\/p>\n

As an electronics store.<\/p>\n

It orginally opened on October 10, 1910 and was the first building in Los Angeles built as a movie theater.<\/p>\n

The Cameo ran movies from 1910 until December 3, 1991 and has been used as an electronics store ever since.  When I toured the theater last year the screen and the speaker behind it were still there, which is very uncommon. <\/p>\n

Just makes me sad every time I pass it.<\/p>\n
