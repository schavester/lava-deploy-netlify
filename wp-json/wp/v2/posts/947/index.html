On February 21, 1925, The New Yorker published its first issue.<\/p>\n

On February 21, 2013, Literary Libations and Johnnie \u00e2\u20ac\u0153The Scot\u00e2\u20ac\u009d Mundell will ply you with whisky in exchange for your words (plus $25)<\/p>\n

We can\u00e2\u20ac\u2122t think of a better way to celebrate literary history.<\/p>\n
