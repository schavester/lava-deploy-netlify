Photo Friends (the LAPL Photo Collection’s Friends group) is hosting a casual (free) reception for our members (and potential members–that’s our hint that you can join while you are at the reception). Meet printer John Matkowsky and some of the awesome Photo Friends board while enjoying a little wine & cheese and Ansel Adams’ photography. You can find out all about how the LAPL Photo Collection came into possession of these images (216 total gifted to the collection by Mr Adams), and how this exhibition came about.
