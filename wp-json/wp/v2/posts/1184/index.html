The Los Angeles Historic Theatre Foundation’s first <\/span>All About<\/em> in 2014 will be a special tour showing the work in progress at the Globe Theatre. Don’t miss this rare chance to see a classic theater and movie palace being brought back to life! The new proprietors of the Globe have uncovered architectural details long hidden from view and are going through much time and effort to restore them. You can be among the first to see these uncovered gems, as well as having a chance to explore spaces long closed to the public, as only an LAHTF <\/span>All About<\/em> can do! <\/span><\/div>\n

<\/span><\/div>\n
Warnings:<\/em><\/strong> While the main auditorium level is easily accessible, the walking tours around the theatre involve a lot of stairs, tight spaces and dusty rooms. Also, there are no bathroom facilities on site, so enjoy breakfast before \u00e2\u20ac\u201dor lunch after\u00e2\u20ac\u201d at a local restaurant and use their facilities<\/em><\/span><\/div>\n
