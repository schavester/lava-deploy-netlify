Presque Pr\u00c3\u00aat a Porter<\/strong>:
Join the repurposed revolution!
Live draping and clothing creation using recycled and re-purposed clothing and fabric- taped and draped on YOU by a skilled costumier, A. Laura Brody. <\/p>\n

Improvised aural explosions by the dynamic duo of David O and Ameenah Kaplan, joined by many adventurous volunteers.<\/p>\n

Bring a piece of clothing or fabric to sacrifice to the art and become born anew from its ashes<\/strong>…jeans that never fit? Unfortunate drapes? Bedraggled bed linens? That never-to-be-used bridesmaid dress? All will be re-made and re-worn.
And you will be a living work of art!
Entrance is free, though donations are always welcome…<\/p>\n

Donations of extra materials (or wine for the bar) will get you pre-emptive draping rights. Just saying.<\/p>\n

Held at Beauty is Pain boutique, the counter-culture rock and roll couture house of Pain!
1443 N. Highland Blvd, Hollywood, CA from 8-10 PM on June 19th, 2010.<\/strong><\/p>\n
