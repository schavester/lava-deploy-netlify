The Photographer’s Eye is a free lecture series presented by Photo Friends of the LAPL. It is a lunchtime series featuring photographers talking about their work. Our Aug. 15 presenter is KEVIN KNIGHT who shows and talks about his fan convention portraits. This promises to be a really fun presentation.<\/p>\n

Feel free to bring your lunch.<\/p>\n

Parking is available at 524 S. Flower St. Garage (show your LAPL library card at the Central Library\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s information desk to receive a validation for reduced rates). Handicap accessibility available.<\/p>\n

Formed in 1990, Photo Friends is a nonprofit organization that supports the Los Angeles Public Library\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s Photograph Collection and History & Genealogy Department. Our mission is to improve access to the collections and promote them through public programs, projects, and exhibits. Become a member! The Photographer\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s Eye series for 2012 is generously sponsored by Christy & Steve McAvoy<\/p>\n
