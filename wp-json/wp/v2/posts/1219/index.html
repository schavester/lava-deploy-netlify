Henry Charles Bukowski (August 16, 1920 \u00e2\u20ac\u201c March 9, 1994)<\/strong><\/p>\n

You are cordially invited to join with friends, fans and followers of Charles Bukowski on the 20th anniversary of his death for an evening of poems, toasts and memories.<\/p>\n

The evening is hosted by Richard Schave (Esotouric\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s Haunts of a Dirty Old Man<\/a> bus tour & the De Longpre bungalow preservation campaign<\/a>) and will include readings from five notable Los Angeles poets and friends of the late writer: Dan Fante<\/a>, S.A. Griffin<\/a>, Suzanne Lummis<\/a>, Joan Jobe Smith<\/a> and Fred Voss<\/a>.<\/p>\n

The location for this free event is the King Eddy Saloon, a location celebrated in the fiction of Bukowski\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s idol, John Fante, and located smack on The Nickel, where the young Bukowski came to learn about the world of men. The formal program is scheduled to run from 6:00 – 7:30pm, after which you are encouraged to remain and talk Bukowski into the night.<\/p>\n
