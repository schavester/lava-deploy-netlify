Members of the ARROYO ARTS COLLECTIVE will show you what they’ve been up to recently in a group art show that is the run-up to the DISCOVERY TOUR, which happens later in Nov. Our amazingly talented local artists will exhibit everything from abstracts to landscapes, fiber art to jewelry, sculpture, prints, multimedia, collage, and, well, a lot of other art stuff.<\/p>\n

Northeast Los Angeles’s Arroyo Arts Collective celebrates a milestone 20th annual Discovery Tour of artists’ homes and studios. Every year, over 100 artists and craftspeople who live and work in Eagle Rock, Highland Park, Mount Washington, and neighboring areas participate (yes, a chance to view artists in their native habitats). To preview the tour, a group show will be on view during November’s Second Saturday Gallery Night at Future Studio Gallery–many of the tour artists will show a representative work, so you can start to plan your tour day. The PREVIEW PARTY on Nov. 10 is FREE; the tour on Sunday, Nov. 18 is a mere $10. You can purchase tickets at the Preview Party, online at http:\/\/arroyoartscollect\u00e2\u20ac\u00a6<\/a>, or at Galco’s (5702 York Blvd., Highland Park). This year’s tour will have shuttle buses available with stops at select locations.<\/p>\n

Future Studio Gallery (Home of Chicken Boy & the Chicken Boy Shop) is now open Saturdays noon to 5 pm; we will also be open the day of the tour. <\/strong><\/p>\n
