The opening event of Puppets Retake Northeast Los Angeles,<\/em> A puppetry festival hosted by the Arroyo Arts Collective<\/p>\n

Puppetry requires that the puppeteer create and perform not just the puppet characters, but all the objects, clothes, and furniture that fill the puppet\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s world. The Arroyo Arts Collective wondered what treasures might lurk in the attics and storage units of puppeteers. The Lost World<\/em> will feature set pieces, costumes, and other memorabilia from puppetry in Los Angeles.<\/p>\n

Gallery and Chicken Boy Shop (inside the gallery) open Saturdays noon to 5 pm. The Lost World<\/em> will be on display to April 13.<\/p>\n
