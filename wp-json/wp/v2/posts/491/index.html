Boyle Heights Paranormal Project along with The Linda Vista Hospital Paranormal Collaborative will offer a tour and investigation of this historic location in an effort to provide education and assistance to individuals who harbor an interest in paranormal research.<\/p>\n

Opened in 1904 by the Santa Fe Railroad to serve the healthcare needs of its employees and closed down in 1991, only to be resurrected as a busy filming locale and currently known as one of the most reputedly haunted locations in the world. We invite you to discover Linda Vista’s rich and terrifying history and to discover for yourself what remains…<\/p>\n

$50 per person<\/p>\n
