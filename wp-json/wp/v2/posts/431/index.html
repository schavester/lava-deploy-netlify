Jon Alloway, an accomplished Director\/Editor\/Photographer makes his living within the Hollywood entertainment and advertising machine. An alumnus of USC School of Cinema, he has worked with many well-known directors throughout his 23-year career and received numerous accolades for outstanding filmmaking. He has photographed for Survival Research Laboratories, The Cacophony Society, Robochrist Industries, Cyclecide Bike Rodeo, Robodock, Burning Man, Lollapalooza, Circus Sideshows, and other creative endeavors. <\/p>\n

As an adventurer of experiences beyond the mainstream, a general all around muckraker, miscreant and world class raconteur, Jon has produced numerous illegal events both inside and outside the chaos that defines The Los Angeles Cacophony Society of which he is an eternal member.<\/p>\n

Mr. Alloway is currently completing the much-anticipated dada-documentary, \u00e2\u20ac\u0153Into the Zone!\u00e2\u20ac\u009d about the Suicide Club and Cacophony Society.<\/p>\n
