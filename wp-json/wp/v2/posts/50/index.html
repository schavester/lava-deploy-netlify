Born in Cambridge, England, in 1958, I was brought up in the Church of England, culminating in regular worship and charitable work for the homeless at London\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s famous St. Martin\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s in the Fields church in Trafalgar Square. Having completed a business degree in 1980, I later studied Eastern Philosophy at London\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s School of Economic Science, as well as spending two periods at the New Age spiritual community of Findhorn in the north of Scotland. At the same time, I was introduced to the cosmic teachings of The Aetherius Society that had been founded in London in 1955 by Western Master of Yoga, Dr. George King, becoming a member in 1986. I moved permanently to the Society\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s American Headquarters in Los Angeles in 1990. Two years later, I became a personal assistant to Dr. King, remaining at his side until his passing in July 1997. Since 2001, I have been a director of The Aetherius Society, helping to spread the cosmic teachings that were given by Masters from beyond this Earth through Dr. King.<\/p>\n
