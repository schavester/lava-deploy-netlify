Called “LA’s Best Food Event of 2010,” Artisanal LA is heading to the Westsiiiiiiiide for two delicious days and taking over a 50k SF vacant department store located above Bloomingdale’s off the new third floor Dining Deck at Santa Monica Place!<\/p>\n

\n

Join Us… <\/p>\n

Saturday, April 16th<\/p>\n

11a – 8p<\/p>\n

&<\/p>\n

Sunday, April 17th<\/p>\n

11a – 7p<\/p>\n

\n

The Spring Show Includes…<\/strong><\/p>\n

* Nearly a hundred local, artisanal and handmade food vendors to shop<\/p>\n

* Chef demos, expert led panels and speakers on local honey, craft beer, urban homesteading, and more!<\/p>\n

* Free hands-on workshops and cool handmade activities <\/p>\n

* Hosted beverages from Honest Tea, Dry Soda, Function, POM and Coba <\/p>\n

* Local\/BioDynamic Wine Tasting   * Hosted Apothecary Bar from VeeV<\/p>\n

* Organic spirits tasting with Tru Organic Spirits \/ Green Bar Collective <\/p>\n

* Comfy lounge areas   * Two caf\u00c3\u00a9 areas featuring Daily Dose & Intelligentsia <\/p>\n

* Souvenir copy of our vendor directory <\/p>\n

* A preview of SMP’s The Market before next month’s Grand Opening <\/p>\n

* Hosted 3-Hr Valet (w\/ Dining Deck Validation) and Bike Valet <\/p>\n

* DJ Bryan Davidson   * Photo Booth <\/p>\n

* Partial Proceeds benefit St Vincent Meals on Wheels <\/p>\n
