This summer, get ready to rock! <\/p>\n

The LA Street Food Fest returns with an all-new Summer Tasting Event at The Rose Bowl on Saturday, July 24th!<\/strong><\/p>\n

Sample unlimited signature bites from almost 60 vendors from hot gourmet food trucks, old school carts and stands to celeb chefs and street inspired dishes from LA’s best restaurants – all serving from beautiful tents on the field of the world famous Rose Bowl! <\/p>\n

Plus… Stay hydrated ‘n happy with our hosted (er, meaning free with your ticket) Singha beer gardens, delicious mixologist made cocktails, tequila tasting,m juices and cold beverages. To top it all off, rock out with live performances from two of LA’s coolest new bands Warpaint and The Deadly Syndrome, all on the grassy green field of the Rose Bowl for one affordable, all-inclusive ticket price benefiting St Vincent Meals on Wheels & Woolly School Gardens!<\/p>\n

* Leave your worries at home with ample FREE parking at the Rose Bowl for ticket-holders<\/p>\n

* Cast a vote for your favorite bite with a People’s Choice Award in the Street Food Cook-Off led by Top Chef Master, Susan Feniger<\/p>\n

* Sip a cold beer and a signature cocktail specially concocted by one of LA’s hottest mixologists and stop by the tequila tasting tent <\/p>\n

* Enjoy live performances from local LA bands The Deadly Syndrome and Warpaint, and a set from DJ Bryan Davidson. JustinTimberlake.com says “they rock”<\/p>\n

* Skip the lines and crowds with limited pre-sale tickets and mingle with just a few thousand of your closest friends in a world famous stadium built for 90,000+<\/p>\n

* Soak up the dog days of summer sun on a blanket on the field, sampling bites at a picnic table or stretched out enjoying the views from one of the upper decks<\/p>\n

* Support your community, local economy and small biz owners<\/p>\n

* Strike a pose at the Oh! Snap photo booth or get a shot with your favorite food truck parked on our red carpet in the Court of Champions<\/p>\n

* Get up close and personal with event beneficiaries St Vincent Meals on Wheels and Woolly School Gardens<\/p>\n

* Go green with us… No bottled water or plastic in the house!<\/p>\n

* All of this and more included in your ticket price!!!<\/p>\n

…..<\/p>\n
