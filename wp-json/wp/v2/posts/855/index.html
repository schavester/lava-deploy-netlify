It’s time for the 10th annual gathering of the Robert Benchley Society. This national group holds its annual Robert Benchley Awards dinner in a new city each year. This year’s gathering is in Los Angeles, home to Mr. Benchley and Mrs. Dorothy Parker from the thirties through the fifties. This year the Society will gather for various activities the weekend of October 12th, including an Awards dinner hosted by <\/span>“LAUGH IN” star, <\/span>Arte  Johnson, at a private club in Santa Monica on Saturday, October 13th. <\/span> Evening dress or 20s-30s attire required at the Awards dinner. <\/span>Must purchase ticket in advance. <\/span><\/p>\n
