Born in Malta, Nicholas grew up in England, the Middle East, Scotland and Australia and arrived in Los Angeles in 1997 through the unusual career path of oil rig engineer to finance broker to computer programmer to actor.  He has worked in Studio Features and Indy Films, Network TV and Theatre.  He has also written and directed short films, and Co-Founded the Moveable Theatre Company (TMTC) in Los Angeles with Holly Witham.  His most recent project for TMTC was directing Mary Casey’s prize winning play ‘Surprise Valley’ about the author Zane Grey and along with Holly he is currently developing a show set in the 1920’s to be produced in a Historic L.A. residence.<\/p>\n
