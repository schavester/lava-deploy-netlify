Official Observance of the 101st Anniversary of the Palace Theatre<\/p>\n

In the first half of 2011, the Palace closed its doors and underwent an amazing transformation. Artisans and craftsmen peeled back years of alterations and wear and tear to reveal the beautiful theatre hidden underneath. The restoration was achieved under the leadership of one of the Palace owners, Shahram Delijani of the Broadway Theatre Group, and general contractor Ed Kelsey.<\/p>\n

Ed Kelsey will share the story of the \u00e2\u20ac\u0153six month miracle\u00e2\u20ac\u009d through before and after photos. Several of the artisans and technicians who did the restoration work will be present to answer your questions and explain the techniques employed.<\/p>\n

A behind-the-scenes tour \u00e2\u20ac\u201c from the basement to the balcony \u00e2\u20ac\u201c will provide an opportunity to see the \u00e2\u20ac\u0153new\u00e2\u20ac\u009d Palace as well as the work yet to be done.<\/p>\n
