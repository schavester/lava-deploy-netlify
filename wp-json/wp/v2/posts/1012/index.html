We are excited to partner with Room & Board for an exhibition showcasing the work of ECF Art Center\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s professional artists. This exciting event will feature dozens of ECF Art Center pieces that have been hand selected to compliment Room & Board Culver City\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s showrooms. The evening will include wine tasting provided by Cameron Hughes and plenty of appetizers. All of the art is available for purchase and 10 percent of the wine sales goes to ECF. Space is limited so please RSVP.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

ECF Art Centers<\/a><\/p>\n

The Art Centers, founded in 1968, currently mentor and represent over 150 adult artists with developmental disabilities. The Art Centers operate four studios Los Angeles County and are part of the larger ECF<\/a> nonprofit organization.<\/p>\n

Room & Board<\/a><\/p>\n

Established over 30 years ago in Minnesota, Room & Board offers beautiful and thoughtful designs for home furnishing. Natural materials are shaped, welded and woven by dedicated craftspeople across the United States.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Cameron Hughes<\/a><\/p>\n

Established by Cameron Hughes and Jessica Kogan, Cameron Hughes wines partners with the finest growers and wineries in the world and delivers consistently high quality wine.<\/p>\n
