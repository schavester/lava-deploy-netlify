Los Angeles through the Lens of Chuck Boyd 1965-1969<\/p>\n

Photographer Chuck Boyd was embedded in the Los Angeles rock & roll scene of the 1960s. A professional photographer, he left behind a collection of nearly 30,000 images of music legends spanning two decades. His photo collection is now overseen by Jeff Schwartz and Chuck\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s siblings.<\/p>\n

Jeff is an award-winning teacher, author, and music historian. He will share images from Chuck\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s archives, discuss \u00e2\u20ac\u0153rock & roll archaeology,\u00e2\u20ac\u009d and talk about Chuck\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s life and the challenges of directing a photo archive.<\/p>\n

This free talk is a repeat performance of Jeff’s 2013 standing room only presentation, presented by Photo Friends of the LAPL and generously sponsored by Christy & Stephen McAvoy<\/p>\n
