LAVA visionary A. Laura Brody is participating in the Sugar Goes Steampunk fashion show! It’s her first fashion show (which features her clothing, that is)- and she has much more planned than just a walk down the runway….<\/p>\n

Come on out and play! There will be live draping, both on stage and off and a whirlwind of several designers.<\/p>\n

Sugar is a night of collective artistry supporting emerging and underground talent, gathering different genres of artists from music, fashion, photography, fine, and graffiti art into one venue. <\/p>\n

Take a trip in our time machine and go Steampunk. We will be exploring the fascinating Steampunk culture, but in an innovative and Sugar kind of fashion. The night will begin at 8pm with an artist and photography showcase, followed by a fashion show that kicks off at 10:30pm featuring 15 emerging and underground local fashion and accessory designers.<\/p>\n

To top the evening off we will have a live all female sword fighting performance by the ladies of Sword Fighting Inc, the sexy 5 gal dance troupe Cirque de Femmes, and our favorite dude on the turntables DJ Spacecadet Adam to create that party vibe. If that isn’t enough to get your heart racing, we have a special solo performance by Jamie Scoles of Nylon Pink!!! To remain true to the Sugar DNA the night will be full of fun and shocking surprises!!!<\/p>\n
