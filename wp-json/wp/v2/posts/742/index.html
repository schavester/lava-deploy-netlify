“Fashion of the Fly- Learn to Staple Drape”
(high school age and up)<\/p>\n

Instructor: LAVA member A. Laura Brody
dreamsbymachine.etsy.com

A detailed how to staple drape your own top, vest or coat with a basic lowdown on costume history shown through draping. Understand costume and clothing terms in plain English, and create a real garment you staple draped yourself! Students come out of the class with a pair of scissors, a stapler, and their very own top. You’ll get simple definitions of pattern and clothing terms and learn how to treat the body as sculpture.<\/p>\n

And if folks are interested, we can talk about how this can become a career. <\/p>\n

$45.00, materials included (this is 1\/2 price!)

11:00am – 3:00pm (12:30 – 1:30 lunch break)<\/p>\n
