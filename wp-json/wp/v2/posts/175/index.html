David Caldwell landed in the American Hotel in 1993, an escapee from an alternate reality known as Santa Barbara California. He immersed himself in the zeitgeist of the Arts District, showing his Multi-Media sculptures at the DADA Shows, the Spanish Kitchen, Factory Place, 50 Bucks, Santa Fe Art Colony, and a host of other venues.<\/p>\n

His annual Halloween Parties with the Seaton Street Gang are still vaguely remembered by downtown revelers.<\/p>\n

He is currently installed in a large loft downtown where he regularly presents War Child Shows,<\/em> a fully immersive Multi-Media Experience.<\/p>\n

He is also the Landscape manager at CBS Television City where he Designs, Installs and Maintains; Perennial Gardens, Roof Gardens,
Succulent Gardens, Ponds and Waterfalls, and more.<\/p>\n

He is currently working on his new movie, Destroy All Dreams.<\/em><\/p>\n
