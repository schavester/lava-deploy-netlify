Individual portraits of Los Angeles\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s new elected officials interpreted by Los Angeles artists<\/p>\n

Opening Saturday, July 13, 2013 \u00e2\u20ac\u00a2 7 to 10 pm
(Second Saturday Gallery Night, Northeast LA)
The exhibit will be on view to Sept. 13
Open Saturdays noon to 5 pm, and by appointment (323-254-4565)<\/p>\n

Our new city government is sworn into office July 1, 2013, and we\u00e2\u20ac\u2122re pretty sure they\u00e2\u20ac\u2122ll be all fired up and ready to go! We are taking this opportunity to congratulate them and  to remind them of the importance of art in the city\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s cultural landscape, personality, and sensibility, not to mention its economy. There\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s so much to love about Los Angeles, but we all know things could be so much better. <\/p>\n
