Samba Society returns to Tr\u00c3\u00b3pico de Nopal for our first outdoor samba event of the year. Bring your dancing feet and get up close to the “roda<\/em>” – the intimate circular formation of samba played in its most traditional format – for a set of samba, batuque, partido alto<\/em>, and samba de terreiro<\/em>.<\/span><\/p>\n

Ten musicians strong, Samba Society brings its own brand of vintage “roots” samba reminiscent of classic recordings from the 1970s. The group features the iconic instrumentation of the 7-string guitar and cavaco, along with full vocal harmonies and swinging percussion.<\/p>\n

Lets celebrate the rites of spring with good Samba and deep roots music… <\/p>\n

REYES spinning Samba, Samba Enredo, Partido Alto, Pagode & Bossa between sets!<\/p>\n
