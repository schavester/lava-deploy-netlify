GHOULA<\/strong> meets for cocktails in haunted places on the 13th of each month. \u00e2\u20ac\u0153SPIRITS with SPIRITS<\/strong>\u00e2\u20ac\u009d is a casual gathering of regional ghost hunters and those interested in ghost stories. This month’s gathering will be at the historic, fairy-tale themed Tam O’Shanter’s Inn, which was not only Walt Disney’s favorite bar (and was one of the local inspirartions for Disneyland), but is also currently home to a few colorful ghosts.<\/p>\n

Note<\/strong>: At this month’s “Spirits with Spirits,” GHOULA will be celebrating the release of Ghoula Press’ first book, Disneyland after Dark: An Unauthorized Guide to the Happiest (Haunted) Place on Earth<\/strong><\/em>. The book will be available for purchase, and the author will be present.<\/p>\n
