Breath is life! Our breathing affects all aspects of our life, for better or for worse, and if we can control our breathing we can control our life.<\/p>\n

Benefits of controlled yoga breathing include:<\/p>\n

    \n
  1. Increased vitality and psychic ability <\/li>\n
  2. Control of stress <\/li>\n
  3. More positive approach to life <\/li>\n
  4. Mental and emotional balance <\/li>\n
  5. More restful sleep <\/li>\n
  6. Greater concentration and memory <\/li>\n
  7. Stronger awareness of our Divine self <\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

    In this practical workshop you will learn a simple yet powerful set of 6 yoga breathing exercises devised by Master of Yoga, Dr. George Kings (1919-1997), that can easily be performed by anyone. We will give you the tools and training you need to include pranayama in your daily life and supercharge it!<\/p>\n
