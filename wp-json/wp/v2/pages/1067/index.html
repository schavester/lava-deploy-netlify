Support<\/h1>\n

LAVA is an independent arts and culture organization that operates without funding from grants, corporate sponsorships or membership fees.  If you enjoy what LAVA brings to your life and to the culture of Los Angeles through the free Sunday Salons, walking tours and other events, please consider supporting LAVA in the following ways:<\/p>\n

• Click this LAVA affiliate link<\/a> before browsing Amazon, or order one of Kim’s books, like the new How To Find Old Los Angeles: A Travel Guide<\/em><\/a>. (When you make a purchase after clicking a link, we will receive a small percentage as an affiliate’s share.)<\/p>\n

• Drop something into our digital Tip Jar<\/a>.<\/p>\n

• Tell your friends about LAVA, and invite them to subscribe<\/a> to the newsletter.<\/p>\n

• Attend ticketed LAVA-exclusive events, such as the Forensic Science Seminars, or the Esotouric bus tours that we host and post on LAVA. For information about upcoming events, click the links at right.<\/p>\n

We’re grateful for your participation in the LAVA community, and appreciate your support.<\/p>\n
