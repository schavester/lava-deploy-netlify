Welcome to LAVA<\/span>, the Los Angeles Visionaries Association. Whether you\u00e2\u20ac\u2122re a Visionary yourself, or just seeking compelling experiences in this city, you\u00e2\u20ac\u2122re in the right place.<\/p>\n

Are you an event promoter, artist, writer or performer who would like to be part of the LAVA<\/span> community? Visit our Application<\/a> page for more info.<\/p>\n

Are you interested in receiving weekly email announcements of LAVA<\/span>-sponsored events? Subscribe<\/a> to our newsletter.<\/p>\n

Los Angeles is a city without a center, but with an unjustly bad reputation. It\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s also home to fascinating people, places and happenings. But these wonders are dotted over a vast and confusing landscape, drowned out by media blare and corporate blather. You could easily spend years in hard searching to discover the real Los Angeles, those hidden gems and secret gatherings that give this city a soul.<\/p>\n

LAVA<\/span> is the key to the secret Los Angeles you\u00e2\u20ac\u2122ve been looking for (or helping to create), and for making connections with people who share your passions and sensibilities. Through participation in LAVA<\/span>, creative Angelenos come together to provide cultural programming that speaks to the urban experience, and contributes to the creation of that rare thing: positive public space.<\/p>\n

Formed by social historians Kim Cooper<\/a> and Richard Schave<\/a>, proprietors of Esotouric<\/a> bus adventures and the 1947project<\/a> series of time travel blogs, LAVA<\/span> brings together the most intriguing creative promoters, artists, writers and thinkers in Los Angeles, sharing their visions for making a better city. Not virtually, though you\u00e2\u20ac\u2122ll want to explore our online calendar<\/a>, but in frequent gatherings of living, breathing, talking human beings, held in select locations all around the town.<\/p>\n

Follow LAVA<\/span>\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s path to find friends you might otherwise never meet. Move through the city, exploring LAVA<\/span> happenings and sharing your feedback. LAVA<\/span> exists to cultivate connections that will excite, inspire and transform the city and its citizens. Our creative partners share a love for L.A. and a unique vision for how to express and explore that love in their work. LAVA<\/span> transforms, and its audience transform LAVA<\/span>.<\/p>\n

Why LAVA<\/span>? We take the image of LAVA<\/span>, the molten rock cast up from the underworld, from the ancient Sankaya Hindu doctrine, which is that of polar opposites driving the world. A ball of lava contains fire and iron in a highly dynamic situation, very close but never mixing. We also see LAVA<\/span> as reflecting all of the tightly coiled, passionate energy that exists in various places and minds within the city: each LAVA<\/span> event provides an opportunity to tap into a well of ideas that in emerging transforms the city and the people who share in the experience.<\/p>\n
