Staple Draping Once More with Fashion on the Fly

A. Laura Brody
0 1.1k
Share

It’s time once more to learn staple draping with Fashion on the Fly!

Staple draping is a fun, fast and effective technique for creating anything made of material. And I mean anything, from upholstery to car covers to clothes.  (No, we will not bring your car into the classroom. Yes, we can drape it in the parking lot.) You’ll learn to make your own shapes and patterns, and even learn how to alter your clothes to make them fit and look better. If enough folks are interested, we’ll do a duct-tape mannequin session. This way you can staple drape yourself at home without straining to reach your own back.

FASHION ON THE FLY CLASS SCHEDULE:

COST: $45 (due at registration) plus $20 materials fee for scissors, fabrics, staples and a stapler.

Instructor: costume creatrix, LAVA Visionary and professional goofball A. Laura Brody, currently working with Nickelodeon and Disney XDTV.

Preview Book Sale Party for Book Lovers

Adrienne Crew
0 878
Share

LAVA Visionary Adrienne Crew is a member of the board of the Mayme Clayton Museum and Library in Culver City, one of the largest archives of African-American books and ephemera in the country. Adrienne invites her fellow LAVA members to join her for an exclusive preview of MCLM’s first sale of duplicate books and periodicals in its collection. Sorry, nothing pre-1948 will be on sale. However, there will be plenty of periodicals, lurid blaxploitation paperbacks and social studies texts on hand.

Come for a glass of wine, buy some books and check out our current exhibit, “Audio Assault: Sights and Sounds of the Black Power Movement 1965-1975,” featuring rare album art, photographs and audio clips that commemorate the events, locations and artists from Southern California that shaped the movement.

Let Adrienne know if you plan to join us by 6/12. Email her at info@adriennecrew.com
